Miles-Bell family history

Bell-Smith

Frederick Bell Elsie Maud Smith Frederick and Elsie Maud were married in 1903, in Kensington, England. The FreeBMD GRO reference is: “Jun 1903, Bell Frederick, Kensington 1a 327” The family emigrated from England to New Zealand sometime after 1908. Text from https://www.myheritage.com/names/elsie_miles has: Frederick was born on November 17 1875, in Alnwick, Northumberland, United Kingdom. Elsie was born in March 1875, in Kensington, London, England. Elsie had 5 brothers: Brian Stephen Bell, Phillip Cecil Bell and 3 other siblings.

Edgar Owen Miles

Elsie Embleton Bell

Elsie born in New Zealand in 1910 - New Zealand certificate ref 1910/3695, to parents Frederick Bell and Elsie Maud Smith. See Miles-Bell for Elsie’s own marriage and family. Elsie died in New Zealand, in 1984, of ovarian cancer.
