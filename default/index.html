Bell - Smith

Frederick Bell Elsie Maud Smith Frederick and Elsie Maud were married in 1903, in Kensington, England. The FreeBMD GRO reference is: “Jun 1903, Bell Frederick, Kensington 1a 327” The family emigrated from England to New Zealand sometime between 1904 and 1909. Text from https://www.myheritage.com/names/elsie_miles has: Frederick was born on November 17 1875, in Alnwick, Northumberland, United Kingdom. Elsie was born in March 1875, in Kensington, London, England.
